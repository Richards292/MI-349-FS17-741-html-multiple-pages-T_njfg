Ultimate Infestation

This is an excellent card, considered one of the strongest cards currently in play. It allows the player to deal 5 damage to their opponent, gain 5 armor, which is similar to health, draw 5 cards, and summon a minion with 5/5 stats (5 health, 5 attack)

This is particularly strong, because it allows the player to "reset" themselves when they've fallen behind. 5 damage allows you to damage an opponent. 5 armor allows you to regain a bit of health. 5 cards lets you fill your hand back up, and a 5/5 ghoul allows you to exert some offensive presence from your own minions.
