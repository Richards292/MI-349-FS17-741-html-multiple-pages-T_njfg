Nourish

This card is yet another one considered at the top of the game. It's purpose is simply to put one player ahead of another in the "mana curve"

Every turn, a player will have access to a "mana supply". The first turn allows the player 1 mana, the second turn 2 mana, up to 10 mana. Cards have a mana cost. This means on turn 1, you can only play cards of 1 mana cost, etc. This serves to limit what cards you can play, and prevents you from playing strong cards too early.

A common strategy is to try to gain mana faster than usual. Some cards will say "gain a mana crystal". This means rather than moving from 1 mana to 2 mana, like usual, they might move from 1 mana to 3 mana. While their opponent is doing the usual 1 to 2. This provides the player with a significant advantage.
